ℹ️ How It Works

📈 False Negatives

📉 False Positives

When the system says "High Risk" but the user marks it as safe, the over-contributing analyzers may need lower weights.

🔒 Read-Only

These suggestions are never auto-applied. They serve as informed recommendations for manual tuning by the administrator.
